ABOUT ADEOLA ADENUGBA.

I am Adeola Adenugba, a Canadian-trained Psychotherapist and Certified Counsellor with a Masters in Marriage and Family Therapy, and Gender Studies. I am a professional member of CAMFT, PACCP, and CCPA. My path to counselling has been driven by a deep commitment to supporting individuals in crisis. As a Mediator in 2017, I found fulfillment in enhancing peaceable relations, but I yearned for deeper, more personal connections in clinical counselling. My passion for helping others led me to a career in psychotherapy, where I support individuals, couples, and families through life's challenges. I believe in providing a judgment-free space where clients can address their deepest concerns. My goal is to foster emotional, mental, and relational well-being, helping my clients navigate life's complexities with compassion and acceptance. If you're facing challenges in any area of your life, you don't have to go through it alone. I'm here to support you on your journey.
